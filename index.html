Our Story

Established in 1982

Cohunu Koala Park is located on fourteen hectares of natural Australian bushland just 40 minutes from the city of Perth in Western Australia.

Here at Cohunu Koalara Park, we house some of Australia's most unique fauna and flora, including dingoes, kangaroos, emus, deer, and most importantly, koalas.
